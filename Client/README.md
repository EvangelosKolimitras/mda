
# MDA

----------

MDA is just another acronym that I came up myself as you might guess.

It's a stack of various technologies:

- MERN
  - [MongoDB]
  - [ExpressJs]
  - [ReactJs]
  - [NodeJs]
- [Docker]
- [AWS]

> Feel free to contribute your own ideas if you find this approach interesting and useful.
